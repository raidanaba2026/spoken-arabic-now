About This Website

Spoken Arabic Now is dedicated to teaching Egyptian Spoken Arabic, not formal or textbook Arabic. Our goal is to help foreigners communicate naturally using the same expressions Egyptians use every day.

We focus on conversation, pronunciation, and real-life phrases — the Arabic you hear on the street, in cafes, and in daily life.

What You Will Learn

Everyday Conversations

Real Egyptian phrases used in daily situations, not formal Arabic.

Egyptian Pronunciation

Learn how Egyptians actually pronounce words, sounds, and letters.

Speak with Confidence

Stop translating in your head and start speaking naturally.

Why Egyptian Arabic?

Egyptian Arabic is the most widely understood dialect in the Arab world. Learning it allows you to communicate with millions of people across different countries.

This platform teaches you how Egyptians really speak — simple, fast, and natural.
